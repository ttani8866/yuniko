# Image Prompt for NanoBananaPro: "The Visual Epic of Awakening"

**Concept:**
A panoramic or triptych composition illustrating the protagonist's journey from a gray corporate hell, through a warm winter sanctuary, to a digital sky of infinite possibility.

**Prompt (English):**
```
(Masterpiece), (Best Quality), (Ultra Detailed), (8k resolution), (Cinematic Lighting),

[Composition]: A triptych narrative flow from left to right.

[Left - The Despair]: 
A cramped, desaturated Japanese office, piles of paperwork, blue light from computer screens, a tired office worker with head in hands, dark gray and cold blue color palette, claustrophobic atmosphere, (Plan Fallacy), (Parkinson's Law), heavy shadows.

[Center - The Sanctuary]:
Transitioning into a warm, cozy Japanese living room, (Kotatsu) with orange blanket, peeled mandarins (Mikan) on top, soft steam rising from a hot cocoa mug, warm sunlight filtering through shoji screens, (Hygge), relaxed atmosphere, soft golden lighting, depth of field.

[Right - The Awakening]:
The scene explodes into a vibrant digital sky, the office worker is now floating/flying, wearing glowing translucent wings made of binary code and neon lights, (Vibe Coding), controlling data streams like a conductor, futuristic city below, bright neon colors (cyan, magenta, electric purple), expression of pure joy and freedom, (Singularity), dynamic pose.

[Style]:
Mix of Cyberpunk and Ghibli-esque warmth, hyper-realistic textures, dramatic contrast between the cold left and the vibrant right.
```

**Negative Prompt:**
```
(worst quality), (low quality), (normal quality), lowres, bad anatomy, bad hands, text, signature, watermark, username, blurry, missing limbs, effortless, cheap, grayscale (except for the left side), dull, boring.
```

**Parameters (Suggested):**
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 21:9 (Ultrawide to show the timeline)
- **Model:** NanoBananaPro v1 (or latest)
- **Guidance Scale:** 7.5
